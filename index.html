02 — Philosophy

Core Manifesto

"Like the samurai blade, every TSURAGI is stripped of the unnecessary. Each element serves a purpose. Every curve has function."

01

Blade Precision

Every automobile designed as a precision instrument. Not "beautiful form" — form as engineering function.

  • Sharp body geometry
  • Pressure-managed aero
  • Tension-driven surfacing
02

Silent Aggression

TSURAGI doesn't scream. It looks dangerous without cheap visual noise. Luxury meets intimidation.

Lamborghini = theatrical aggression
Ferrari = emotional sculpture
TSURAGI = disciplined weapon
03

Human-Centered Tech

Even in 2035, TSURAGI doesn't build "rolling smartphones." The driver remains the center of the machine.

  • Tactile metal controls
  • Haptic interfaces
  • Analog emotional feedback
04

Japanese Minimal Brutalism

Inspired by samurai armor, katana geometry, Tokyo architecture, and Shinkansen aerodynamics.
